Antipasto Garlic Bread

(Source)

Ingredients

  • 2 x 22cm-long baguette breads
  • 180g Alfa One rice bran oil spread
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped pitted kalamata olives
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped semi-dried tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp finely chopped fresh chives
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped fresh basil leaves

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C/180°C fan-forced. Slice each baguette diagonally at 1.5cm intervals, being careful not to cut all the way through.
  2. Combine spread, garlic, olives, tomato, chives and basil in a bowl. Season with pepper. Spread both sides of each bread slice wit spread mixture. Enclose baguettes tightly in foil. Place on a baking tray. Bake for 10 to 15 minutes or until hot and crisp. Slice and serve.
