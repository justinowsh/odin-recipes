Caramel Slice

(Source)

Ingredients

Base

  • 150g flour, plain/all-purpose
  • 80g brown sugar, loosely packed
  • 45g desiccated coconut
  • 125g unsalted butter

Caramel Filling

  • 125g unsalted butter, roughly chopped
  • 80g brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract or essence
  • 395g sweetened condensed milk

Chocolate topping

  • 200g dark or milk melting chocolate
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions

Base

  1. Preheat oven to 180째C (fan 160째C)
  2. Grease and line a 28 x 18cm (lamington pan) with baking/parchment paper. Have overhang for ease of removal.
  3. Mix together Base ingredients and press into a pan
  4. Bake for 15 minutes until the surface is golden. Cool in fridge if you have time

Caramel

  1. Lower oven to 160째C (fan 140째C)
  2. Place butter, sugar and vanilla in a saucepan over medium low heat. When the butter is melted, whisk to combine with sugar, then just leave it until it comes to a simmer.
  3. When bubbles appear, add condensed milk. Whisk constantly for 5 minutes, until some big slow bubbles form on the base.
  4. Once bubbles start appearing, whisk for 1 minute, then pour onto Base. Tilt pan to spread evenly.
  5. Bake for 12 minutes.
  6. Cool on counter for 20 minutes then refrigerate for 30 minutes - bottom of pan should be warm but surface cool to touch.

Chocolate

  1. Place chocolate and oil in a microwave proof bowl. Microwave in 30 second intervals, stirring in between, until chocolate is fully melted.
  2. Pour over caramel, spread with spatula. Gently shake pan to even out the surface.
  3. Refrigerate for 1 hour or until set. Remove from fridge and leave out for 5 minutes to take chill out of chocolate slightly. Then cut into squares to serve!
